1. Executive Summary

inscene.io is a category-disrupting AI curator for the global stock footage industry `[1]`. It replaces fragmented keyword-based search with a Narrative Thought Engine. By utilizing a "Paragraph-to-Shot" discovery interface and a unique Reasoning-Rebuttal Loop, the platform provides a high-utility collaborative workflow for professional editors while maintaining a zero-liability profile as a non-hosting intermediary `[1]`.

2. AI-First Discovery: Paragraph-as-Thought

Unlike traditional RAG systems that rely on flat vector similarity, inscene.io employs a Situation Modeling approach via the NARRABENCH framework. The engine decomposes paragraphs into twelve narrative features across four dimensions: Story (motivations), Narration (perspective), Discourse (chronological chains), and Situatedness (intent) `[1]`.

3. Reasoning-Rebuttal Loop (XAI)

To meet the Explainable AI (XAI) mandates of 2026, every recommendation is accompanied by a "Logic Receipt" mapping input to Schankian Primitives (e.g., PTRANS for physical movement) `[1]`. Thought Signatures preserve the reasoning state without re-tokenizing the entire history during refinements `[1]`.

4. Multi-Provider Orchestration (MCP)

Agentic workflows are managed via MCP servers, allowing for the registration of multiple provider endpoints (Shutterstock, Pond5, iStock) at initialization `[1]`. We adopt the IPTC Video Metadata Hub v1.7 to normalize data and distinguish between generative and traditional footage `[1]`.

5. The "Vault" Model & Security

Anonymized user rebuttals are stored in "The Vault"—a secure graph store preserving the causal logic of expert corrections `[1]`. Honeytoken security triggers an immediate session kill-switch if accessed by compromised agents. We offload 90% of routine compute to the edge using Gemma 4 `[1]`.

6. Marketplace Neutrality & Ranking

Our ranking formula prioritizes relevance and validation over affiliate yield `[1]`. The Redundancy Factor ($F_{Redundancy}$) acts as a guardrail preventing single-provider dominance `[1]`.

7. Regulatory Alignment

Architecture compliant with EU AI Act (Art 12/13), FTC AI Policy (2026), and NIST AI RMF via Logic Receipts and Vault sanitization `[1]`.
