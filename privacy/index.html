Entity Identification & Scope

inscene.io (the "Company") acts as the Data Fiduciary under the India Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023 and the Data Controller under the GDPR. At this beta stage, we collect only your name and email address for waitlist management and service communication.

AI-Specific Data Handling: Edge Sanitization

To ensure "Data Protection by Design," the Company utilizes Edge Sanitization Routing. Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and creative briefs are processed locally via on-device deployments; only anonymized, abstract semantic vectors are scaled to our cloud for discovery reasoning.

The Sovereign Compliance Vault

While raw media pixels are ephemeral and flushed from memory post-inference, the Company maintains a Sovereign Compliance Vault. This vault secures reasoning traces and "Logic Receipts"—cryptographic records of system reasoning—for the mandatory window required by the 2026 IT Rules in India and transparency mandates in the EU.

Data Principal Rights & Erasure

Under the DPDP Act and GDPR, you have the absolute Right to Erasure. Valid requests for the deletion of your personal data will be fulfilled within 7 days, subject to our statutory legal obligations to maintain reasoning traces for law enforcement.
